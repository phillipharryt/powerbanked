There are a wide variety of powerbanks with varied charge capacities (measured in mAh) and uses. Below is a guide detailing which Power Bank should suit your needs.

≤5000mAh
Portability over power. These are for topping up your device throughout the day. Able to recharge a phone once or twice before it requires recharging. Great for fitting in a pocket or purse.

5000-10,000mAh
Perfect for recharging several devices throughout your week, or one phone up to 10 times! Sizeable amount of power in a not-so-sizeable package.

10,000+mAh
For the real power users! The Power Bank for people who need some serious juice! Fill one of these bad boys up and your tablet or phone won't see a low battery warning for weeks. A veritable powerplant in your pocket.

Solar Powered
The Power Bank for the user who likes the outdoors. Trades convenience for the ability to charge through the power of the sun! Harness light to charge your phone, so not perfect for those living in England.
